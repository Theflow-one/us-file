Our Story

Welcome to R&K Auto! Established in February 2018 by Ralph Carmichael, our lead mechanic, along with his dedicated partners—his wife and his brother—R&K Auto has become a trusted name in automotive care.

With over 30 years of hands-on experience, Ralph turned a lifelong dream into reality, starting this family-owned business with a clear vision and commitment to excellence.

Our motto, "Your Car, Our Business," reflects our dedication to customer satisfaction and vehicle safety. We believe in honest, transparent service and building long-term relationships with our clients.
